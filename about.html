About ArtisanCraft India

Preserving India's rich cultural heritage while empowering artisan communities

Home / About

Our Story

ArtisanCraft India was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that traditional handicrafts are not just products, but living expressions of our cultural soul. Every piece tells a story of generations of skill, passion, and heritage that deserves to be celebrated and preserved.

We bridge the gap between India's master artisans and the global community, ensuring that these timeless crafts continue to thrive in the modern world while providing sustainable livelihoods to the skilled hands that create them.

Preserving Heritage Since 2020

Our Mission

To preserve and promote India's traditional handicrafts by creating sustainable market opportunities for artisans, ensuring fair compensation, and connecting authentic handmade products with conscious consumers worldwide.

  • Empower artisan communities through fair trade practices
  • Preserve traditional crafting techniques for future generations
  • Create sustainable livelihoods in rural and semi-urban areas
  • Promote authentic Indian handicrafts globally

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted platform for authentic Indian handicrafts, where every purchase supports an artisan family and contributes to preserving India's rich cultural heritage for generations to come.

  • A thriving ecosystem of empowered artisan communities
  • Global recognition for Indian traditional crafts
  • Sustainable employment in rural India
  • Cultural heritage preserved through commerce

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Authenticity

Every product is genuinely handcrafted using traditional techniques passed down through generations. We guarantee the authenticity of every piece.

Fair Trade

We ensure artisans receive fair compensation for their work, promoting sustainable livelihoods and economic empowerment in rural communities.

Sustainability

Our practices support environmental sustainability through eco-friendly materials, traditional techniques, and minimal industrial processing.

Cultural Preservation

We actively work to preserve India's rich cultural heritage by supporting traditional crafts and passing skills to new generations.

Quality Excellence

We maintain the highest standards of quality, ensuring every product meets our rigorous criteria for craftsmanship and durability.

Global Reach

We connect local artisans with global markets, sharing India's cultural treasures with conscious consumers worldwide.
