Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I place a bulk order?

For bulk orders, please contact us directly at hello@artisancraft.in or call +91 98765 43210. We offer special pricing for orders above â‚¹50,000 and can customize products according to your requirements.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Please contact us for specific shipping information to your country.

How can I become an artisan partner?

We're always looking for skilled artisans to join our community. Please visit our "Join the Community" page or contact us with samples of your work and details about your craft specialization.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original condition. Due to the handmade nature of our products, slight variations are normal and not grounds for return unless there's a quality issue.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely! Every product comes with an authenticity certificate. We work directly with artisans and have strict quality control measures to ensure all items are genuinely handcrafted using traditional techniques.

Can I visit your workshop or showroom?

Yes! Our showroom is open Monday-Saturday, 9 AM-6 PM. We also organize workshop visits by appointment. Please call ahead to schedule your visit and ensure availability.
